My ELO is 900

This page is as much for me as it is for anyone reading this, so I can remember the fundamentals that may or may not get me to 1000.

  1. Develop pieces and castle as soon as possible
  2. Connect your rooks
  3. Rooks on the 7th rank are dangerous
  4. Look for checks, captures, and attacks
  5. Count attackers and defenders
  6. Protect your pawns
  7. Bishop pairs are strong- don't trade them for knights
  8. Be wary of back-rank checkmates
  9. When you're up material, play carefully and slowly
  10. Forks, pins, knifes, and batteries are ways to go up material
